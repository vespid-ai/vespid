# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but focus on verifiable technical outcomes.
- Accept feedback professionally.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks, doxxing, or threats.
- Deliberate disruption of project collaboration.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and interactions that violate this policy.

## Reporting
Contact conduct@vespid.ai for Code of Conduct incidents.
